Tech Rally Pushes All Indices Higher as Chip Stocks Stage Comeback

The S&P 500 advanced 0.89% to close at 7,509.20, the Nasdaq Composite added 1.29% to end at 25,837.21, and the Dow gained 0.74% to 52,224.64. All three indexes broke three-day losing streaks. Wall Street staged a comeback as dip buyers emerged, with investors piling into beaten-down semiconductor firms, where a key sector gauge rose 5.2%.
