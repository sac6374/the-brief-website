What is The Brief?

The markets move every day. Your career depends on understanding why.

The Brief is a free daily newsletter written for finance students and early-career professionals — with a Wealth Management and Private Banking angle. Every weekday morning, we break down what moved markets, why it mattered, and exactly what to say about it in your next interview or client conversation.

No noise. No recycled headlines. Just the chain of logic from markets to your career.

Interview-Ready Takeaways

We don't just report what happened. We follow the logic: event → market reaction → second-order effects → career implications. That chain of reasoning is what separates candidates who read the news from candidates who understand markets.
